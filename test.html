Elizabeth Bennet Test Chat

 

This is a test application for the Elizabeth Bennet Chatbot.

The test application is based upon the original example given by Joseph Weizenbaum in his article on ELIZA from Jannuary 1966 [1].
Click "Next Step" to see the next input and Elizabeth's answer.
Click "Reset" to reset the chatbot and the display form.


Here is a transcript of the conversation shown.
The capitalized lines are Elizabeth's responses.

Men are all alike.

IN WHICH WAY, DEAR FRIEND?

They're always bugging us about something or other.

MAY I ASK, CAN YOU THINK OF A SPECIFIC EXAMPLE?

Well, my boyfriend made me come here.

DEAR FRIEND, WHY DO YOU SAY YOUR BOYFRIEND MADE YOU COME HERE?

He says I'm depressed much of the time.

I AM SORRY TO HEAR YOU ARE DEPRESSED. YOU MUST WISH, AS WELL AS EVERYBODY ELSE, TO BE PERFECTLY HAPPY; BUT, LIKE EVERYBODY ELSE, IT MUST BE IN YOUR OWN WAY.

It's true.
I am unhappy.

DO YOU BELIEVE THAT COMING HERE WILL HELP YOU NOT TO BE UNHAPPY, DEAR FRIEND?

I need some help, that much seems certain.

THE TRUE PERFECTION OF A PERSON LIES NOT IN WHAT THEY HAVE, BUT WHAT THEY ARE.
WHAT WOULD IT MEAN TO YOU IF YOU GOT SOME HELP?

Perhaps I could learn to get along with my mother.

YOU MUST TELL ME MORE ABOUT YOUR FAMILY.

My mother takes care of me.

WHO ELSE IN YOUR FAMILY TAKES CARE OF YOU? THERE ARE PEOPLE, WHO THE MORE YOU DO FOR THEM,
THE LESS THEY WILL DO FOR THEMSELVES.

My father.

YOUR FATHER? DEAR FRIEND, PLEASE TELL ME IF SO.

You are like my father in some ways.

WHAT RESEMBLANCE DO YOU SEE AT PRESENT?

You are not very aggressive
but I think you don't want me to notice that.

WHAT MAKES YOU BELIEVE THAT I AM NOT VERY AGGRESSIVE, DEAR FRIEND?

You don't argue with me.

WHY MUST YOU BELIEVE THAT I DON'T ARGUE WITH YOU?

You are afraid of me.

DOES IT PLEASE YOU TO BELIEVE I AM AFRAID OF YOU?

My father is afraid of everybody.

WHAT ELSE COMES TO YOUR MIND WHEN YOU THINK OF YOUR FATHER?

Bullies.

DEAR FRIEND, DOES THAT HAVE ANYTHING TO DO WITH THE FACT
THAT YOUR BOYFRIEND MADE YOU COME HERE?
